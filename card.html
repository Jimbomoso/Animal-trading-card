Asian Elephant

Similar to human handedness, elephants have a preferred tusk; they can be either left or right-tusked. However, only left-tusked elephants are in their right mind!

  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta
  • Weight: Up to 12,000lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 60 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands and forrests

The Asian elephant is the largest living land animal in Asia. It is estimated that there are between 40,000 and 50,000 Asian elephants in existance.
